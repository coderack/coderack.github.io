Here’s a simple place to track the books and long-form articles I keep recommending to others.

## Books

- [High Growth Handbook – Product Management Overview](https://growth.eladgil.com/book/chapter-7-product-management/product-management-overview/) — a go-to reference for companies that have moved past 0→1 but still need to mature their product discipline.
- [Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software](https://www.amazon.com/Design-Patterns-Elements-Reusable-Object-Oriented/dp/0201633612) — a classic book on design patterns.

## Articles & Essays


- [The Cadence – How to Operate a SaaS Startup](https://medium.com/craft-ventures/the-cadence-how-to-operate-a-saas-startup-436aa8099e8) — a simple, repeatable process for building product momentum.
- [Fast - By Patrick Collison](https://patrickcollison.com/fast) — Inspiring examples of projects that move fast.
- [The Laws of UX - By Leah Buley](https://lawsofux.com/) — A comprehensive guide to the principles of user experience design.
